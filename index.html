Animal Organ Donor

  • High Concept: Players will get to mess around with different bodies of humans, and animals and make there own wierd creature.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players get to enjoy a bit of customization. By clicking on different objects prompted by the computer the player will be able to customize silly creatures of combined animals. The sillyness of the players creations, and the different customization should give the players many things to laugh about along with lots of replayability.
  • Genre: Animal Organ Doctor is a simple customization game with a bit of a surgeon doctor feel to it. Players will create different creatures giving it the customization feel. With a top down view of the patient players will get the surgeon feel of the game.
  • Game play: Players will be prompted to pick between different body parts throughout the course of the game. As players continue to pick parts eventually they will reach a final screen that asks the player if they are ready to see their creation. When the button is clicked the player will be shown their final creation with each of the body parts in it's select location.
  • Prototype goal: The main goal of this prototype is to once again explore using the mouse click to cause things to occur as well as trying to use different objects in certain areas.This prototype also explores customization, and how it can be used in games.
  • Features: The cool things of Animal Organ Doctor is it includes several choices the player can choose from. For example the player will be able to choose between a Gorilla body, a horses head, giraffe feet, and human arms. Also these choices create great replay value.
  • Setting/Premise: Now in the near future humanity has come to realize the human race is too impared by it's biological setbacks. In order to make up, and improve on this loss, humanity has started to replace human organs with animal organs. Humanity needs your help to choose the correct organs for the newest patient!
  • Story: After many years of evolving the human race only seems to be getting stupider, and stupider. Humanity worried of devolving back into monkeys decided to research ways to prevent this outcome. Looking around the scientists of the world noticed that the animals around them seem to be the best at adapting, evolving, and having great survival skills. Recognizing this fact humanity decides to try to mix animals with humans, in order to create the ultimate human.
  • Target Audience: Animal Organ Doctor appeals to all ages. It's simple humor along with it's not to mild graphics allows it to appeal to all kinds of age groups. Any age player should be able to get a good laugh out of Animal Organ Doctor.
  • Play time: Animal Organ Doctor is a short game that should take no longer than a minute.
  • Strategies: Luckily for the player Animal Organ Doctor is a game about having fun, and trying whatever you want. Because of this there isn't really much of a strategy to Animal Organ Doctor besides wha combo's seem the funniest.
  • Prototype Assets: Most images will be made in microsoft paint or photoshop.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Many of the phaser games along with my old game Nerdy Dating Sim will be used in Animal Organ Doctor.
